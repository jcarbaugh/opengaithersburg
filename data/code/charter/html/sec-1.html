Sec. 1 - Incorporation; city body politic; power to sue and be sued; common seal; perpetual succession.

The inhabitants within the corporate limits heretofore legally established as and for the City of Gaithersburg are hereby continued as a body corporate to be known as the "City of Gaithersburg," with all of the privileges of a body corporate, by that name to sue and be sued, to plead and be impleaded in any court of law or equity, to have and use a common seal and to have perpetual succession.
