Sec. 14 - Mayor-Term of office; when term begins.

The mayor shall be elected as hereinafter provided and except as hereinafter provided shall hold office for a term of four (4) years or until his successor shall take office. Each mayor hereafter elected shall take office on the first Monday following his election. The next election for the office of mayor shall take place in 1986 with a subsequent election in 1989 and every fourth year thereafter.
