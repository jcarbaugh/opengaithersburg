Sec. 4 - Same-Qualifications of councilmen.

No person shall be eligible to the office of councilman unless that person is domiciled in the city immediately preceding their election and unless that person is also a qualified voter of the city at the time of their election. All council members must continue their domicile in the city during their term in office.
