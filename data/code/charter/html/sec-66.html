Sec. 66 - Powers of council as to public ways.

The council shall have power:

  1. To regulate the grades, dimensions, materials and manner of installation of any pavement, bridge, curb, gutter, sidewalk or other improvement constructed in a public way.

  2. To establish and name public ways or widen or extend existing public ways.

  3. To grade and pave public ways and construct, install, repair or reconstruct therein, bridges, streets, curbs, gutters, sidewalks, drains and other necessary or desirable facilities.

  4. To require the owners of any property abutting on a sidewalk in a public way to keep that sidewalk clear of ice, snow and other obstruction.
