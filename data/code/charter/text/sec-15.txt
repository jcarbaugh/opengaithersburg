# Sec. 15 - Same-Qualifications.

No person shall be eligible for election to the office of mayor unless that person is domiciled in the city for at least one year immediately preceding their election and unless that person is also a qualified voter of the city at the time of their election. The mayor shall continue to be domiciled in the city during the mayor's term in office.
