# Sec. 29 - Filing for elected office.

A person may file for elected office in the city by filing a petition signed by not less than one hundred (100) qualified voters of the city asking that his name be placed upon the ballot for the office which he seeks. Said petition shall be filed at the office of the board of supervisors of elections on or before the close of business on the forty-fifth day preceding the next general election. No person shall seek election to more than one elected city public office at any one time. The council shall, by ordinance, establish procedures to implement this section and to determine compliance with this section and sections 4 and 15 of the Charter.

*(Ord. No. CA-3-77; Res. No. CA-1-82; Ord. No. CA-1-94, 12-19-94)*
