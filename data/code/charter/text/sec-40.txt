# Sec. 40 - Budget-Submission to council; balance required; public record.

The city manager, on such date as the council shall determine, but at least sixty (60) days before the beginning of any fiscal year, shall submit a budget to the council. The budget shall provide a complete financial plan for the budget year and shall contain estimates of anticipated revenues and proposed expenditures for the coming year. The total of the anticipated revenue shall equal or exceed the total of the proposed expenditures. The budget shall be a public record in the city office, open to public inspection by anyone during normal business hours.
