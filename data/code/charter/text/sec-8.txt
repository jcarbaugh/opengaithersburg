# Sec. 8 - Same-Mayor to serve as president; vice president.

The mayor shall serve as president of the council. He may take part in all discussions of the council, but shall have no vote on any decision by the council. The council shall elect a vice president of the council from among its members who shall act as president in the absence of the president who shall be entitled to vote on any decision by the council. At any meeting at which the president and vice president shall both be absent the council may elect one of its members to act as president pro tem who shall be entitled to vote on any decision by the council.

*(Ord. No. CA-5-74)*
