# Sec. 82 - Continuation of ordinances, resolutions, etc.; repeal of conflicting ordinances, resolutions, etc.

1. All ordinances, resolutions, rules, and regulations in effect in the city at the time this Charter becomes effective which are not in conflict with the provisions of this Charter shall remain in effect until changed or repealed according to the provisions of this Charter.

1. All ordinances, resolutions, rules, and regulations in effect in the city at the time this Charter becomes effective which are in conflict with the provisions of this Charter be and the same hereby are repealed to the extent of such conflict.
