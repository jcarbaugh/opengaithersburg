About Open Gaithersburg

The site

Open Gaithersburg is an experiment to make public data published by the city easier to use. The intial focus is on campaign finance data, but may expand to other types of data in the future.

Created by web developer and Gaithersburg resident Jeremy Carbaugh. Please contact me with any and all suggestions for improvements.

The data

All campaign finance data comes directly from the City of Gaithersburg. The city provides downloads of all data collected from required campaign disclosure forms.

Data last updated 9/19/2011. New data is released within a few days of the filing deadlines:

The technology

Open Gaithersburg is open source. All contributions are welcomed!

Data is loaded and processed using saucebrush. Addresses are geocoded with the Yahoo! PlaceFinder API. Graphs and charts are generated using Google Chart Tools, maps with the Google Maps API. This site is powered by the Flask web framework and uses Twitter's Bootstrap UI toolkit.
